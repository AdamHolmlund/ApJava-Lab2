About:

Written in 2014 by Adam Holmlund

Information about travel offers are retrieved from
http://www.fritidsresor.se/Blandade-Sidor/feeds/tradera/ and presented in a 
table. Double-click on the offer you wish to see more about and a window will 
appear where you can view more information and where you can be linked to the 
homepage to order the journey.

You can search for which destination you wish to visit and the search table
will be filtered according to what you write in the search bar. The search
is case sensitive.
